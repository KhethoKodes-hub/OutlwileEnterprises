About Outlwile

Learn about our journey, mission, and commitment to excellence in mining maintenance, transportation, supply services, and events management.

Our Story

Outlwile Trading Enterprise, established in 2019, was born from a vision to combat unemployment among skilled young black South African youth.

Recognizing the untapped potential within our country, we set out to create a business leveraging our expertise in the construction, cleaning, and transport sectors.

As the global demand for minerals and metals escalates, so does the expectation for industries to contribute to socio-economic development while minimizing environmental impact. In response, we embrace a "Future Smart" approach, integrating sustainability into our operations.

Our Sustainability Strategy, shaped by extensive internal and external engagement, aligns with global sustainability goals, including the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Our Mission

At Outlwile Trading Enterprise (Pty) Ltd, our mission is rooted in delivering unparalleled service excellence and reliability across all aspects of our operations.

We prioritize providing quality services coupled with exceptional customer care, ensuring satisfaction for all stakeholders. Our goal is to foster mutually beneficial and rewarding partnerships among the company's directors, members, employees, and, most importantly, our clients.

We are dedicated to offering comprehensive services, striving to complete projects efficiently and within budget. Upholding strict adherence to quality, health, and safety standards, we prioritize labor equity and encourage harmonious personnel relationships to promote teamwork among our staff.

Committed to making a positive impact on our community and the economy, we continually seek opportunities for improvement and sustainable growth.

Our Vision

At Outlwile Trading Enterprise (Pty) Ltd, our vision is to be acknowledged as the foremost provider of Mining Maintenance Services, Transportation, Supplying Services, and Events Management solutions.

We aspire to establish the benchmark for excellence within our industry, guided by our steadfast commitment to integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our aim is to become the hub of excellence, delivering services that catalyze positive, measurable transformations in our customers' business objectives while addressing social challenges and uplifting the communities of the Northern Cape.
